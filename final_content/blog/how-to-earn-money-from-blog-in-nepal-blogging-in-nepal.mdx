## Step 1: Choose the Right Blogging Platform

The platform you choose determines how much control, flexibility, and earning potential your blog has. Here are the two best options for Nepali bloggers in 2025.

### Option A: Blogger.com — Best for Absolute Beginners (Free)

Blogger is a free blogging platform owned by Google. You sign up with your Gmail account and get a free subdomain like `yourblog.blogspot.com`.

**Pros:**
- Completely free — no hosting or domain costs
- Backed by Google, so it is reliable and secure
- Easy to connect with Google AdSense
- No technical knowledge required to get started

**Cons:**
- Limited design customization compared to WordPress
- Your blog lives on Google's servers — you do not fully own it
- The `blogspot.com` subdomain looks less professional
- Fewer plugins and growth tools available

> **Best for:** Students and beginners who want to test blogging before investing money.

---

### Option B: WordPress.org — Best for Serious Bloggers (Recommended)

WordPress.org is the world's most popular blogging and website platform, powering over 43% of all websites globally. It is free software, but you need to pay for hosting and a domain name.

**Pros:**
- Full control over your website design and functionality
- Thousands of free and premium themes and plugins
- Better SEO capabilities with plugins like Yoast SEO or Rank Math
- More professional appearance, which helps with AdSense and brand deals
- You fully own your content and website

**Cons:**
- Requires purchasing hosting (approximately NPR 800–2,000/year) and a domain (approximately NPR 1,200–1,500/year for `.com`)
- Slightly more setup involved than Blogger

> **2025 Hosting Recommendation for Nepal:** Look into **Subisu**, **WebSurfer Nepal**, or **Hostinger** (international, affordable). Many offer Nepal-based payment options including eSewa and Khalti.

## Step 2: Set Up Your Blog

### Setting Up on Blogger (Free)

1. Go to [blogger.com](https://www.blogger.com) and sign in with your Google account.
2. Click **Create New Blog**.
3. Enter a blog title that reflects your niche (e.g., "Nepal Travel Tips" or "Tech Reviews Nepal").
4. Choose a URL — pick something short, memorable, and relevant.
5. Select a template or skip and customize later.

**After setup, do these immediately:**

- Go to **Settings → Basic** and add a clear blog description.
- Go to **Settings → Search Preferences → Meta Tags** and enable the meta description field.
- Add this robots.txt under **Search Preferences → Custom Robots.txt**:
