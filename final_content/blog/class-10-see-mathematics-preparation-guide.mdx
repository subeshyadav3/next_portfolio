## Introduction to SEE Mathematics

The Secondary Education Examination (SEE) Mathematics paper is one of the most important subjects for Class 10 students in Nepal. A strong score in mathematics can significantly boost your overall GPA. This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know to prepare effectively for the 2026 examination.

Mathematics in SEE is not just about memorizing formulas — it is about understanding concepts, recognizing patterns, and applying logical reasoning. Students who focus on conceptual clarity consistently score above 90%.

<InfoBox title="Exam Update 2026">
The CDC has maintained the same syllabus structure for 2026. However, there is an increased emphasis on application-based questions and problem-solving skills in the new curriculum framework.
</InfoBox>

## Essential Formulas with Examples

### Algebra

**Quadratic Equation**

The standard form is ax² + bx + c = 0 where a ≠ 0.

<Definition term="Quadratic Formula">
The solutions of ax² + bx + c = 0 are given by x = [-b ± √(b² - 4ac)] / 2a. The expression b² - 4ac is called the discriminant.
</Definition>

<Example title="Solving a Quadratic Equation">
Solve: x² - 5x + 6 = 0

Here a = 1, b = -5, c = 6

x = [5 ± √(25 - 24)] / 2 = [5 ± √1] / 2

x = (5 + 1)/2 = 3 or x = (5 - 1)/2 = 2

Therefore, x = 3 or x = 2.
</Example>

**Arithmetic Sequence (AP)**

The nth term of an AP: aₙ = a₁ + (n-1)d

Sum of n terms: Sₙ = n/2 [2a + (n-1)d]

<KeyPoints title="AP Formula Quick Reference">
- Common difference d = a₂ - a₁
- If d > 0, sequence is increasing
- If d < 0, sequence is decreasing
- If d = 0, sequence is constant
</KeyPoints>

### Geometry

**Circle Theorems You Must Know**

1. The angle subtended by an arc at the center is twice the angle subtended at any point on the remaining part of the circle.
2. Angles in the same segment of a circle are equal.
3. The angle in a semicircle is a right angle (90°).
4. The tangent at any point of a circle is perpendicular to the radius through the point of contact.

<Example title="Circle Theorem Application">
In a circle with center O, if ∠AOB = 80° (central angle), find ∠ACB where C is any point on the remaining part of the circle.

Solution: Using theorem 1, ∠ACB = ½ × ∠AOB = ½ × 80° = 40°
</Example>

### Mensuration

| Shape | Perimeter / Circumference | Area | Volume |
|-------|--------------------------|------|--------|
| Circle | C = 2πr | A = πr² | — |
| Cylinder | — | CSA = 2πrh, TSA = 2πr(r+h) | V = πr²h |
| Cone | — | CSA = πrl, TSA = πr(r+l) | V = ⅓πr²h |
| Sphere | — | SA = 4πr² | V = ⁴⁄₃πr³ |

<WarningBox title="Common Mistakes in Mensuration">
- Confusing radius with diameter — always halve the diameter
- Forgetting to add the base area in TSA of cylinder and cone
- Mixing up curved surface area with total surface area
- Not writing the correct units (cm² vs cm³)
</WarningBox>

## Common Errors and How to Avoid Them

1. **Sign errors in algebra**: When moving terms across the equals sign, always change the sign. Double-check your sign changes.

2. **Incorrect substitution in formulas**: Write the formula first, then substitute values one by one.

3. **Unit conversion mistakes**: Convert all measurements to the same unit before calculating.

4. **Graph plotting errors**: Label axes clearly and use a proper scale.

## Practice Strategy for High Scores

<TipBox>
Create a formula sheet with all important formulas and review it every morning. This helps with active recall and long-term retention.
</TipBox>

### Step 1: Conceptual Understanding
Read each chapter from the textbook. Do not skip the theoretical explanations.

### Step 2: Solved Examples
Work through every solved example in the textbook. Cover the solution and try to solve it yourself first.

### Step 3: Exercise Problems
Solve all exercise problems at the end of each chapter. Mark difficult ones for revision.

### Step 4: Previous Year Questions
Solve at least 5 years of past SEE mathematics papers. This is the most effective preparation strategy.

### Step 5: Mock Tests
Take full-length mock tests under timed conditions. This builds exam stamina and improves time management.
