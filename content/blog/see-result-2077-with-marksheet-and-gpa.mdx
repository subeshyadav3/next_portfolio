**How to see the result?**

**\->** If you want to see the SEE RESULT then follow the steps.

.1 Go to the search engine and type \*NTC NET RESULT.

2\. OR go to these websites [SEE RESULT 20](http://neb.ntc.net.np/)77

3\. You will see the result processing interface there.

4\. Now, put the _symbol number_ and _date of birth_ in the correct format.

5\. Then click on the _submit button._

 Hence, it will process your information and provide you your result with GPA and also mark sheet if available. If you appear you result wrong and something bad, you can increase your grade doing a recheck. BEST OF LUCK
